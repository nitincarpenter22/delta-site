02

Bottom-Funnel Content Production

We write 3-4 high-intent pages every month — the specific page types that capture visitors who are already in buying mode. These are not blog posts about industry trends. They are commercially focused pages designed to rank for purchase-intent searches and convert readers into trial signups.

Page Type 1
Competitor alternative pages — captures users actively looking to leave a competitor tool
Page Type 2
Comparison pages — "[Your Tool] vs [Competitor]" — captures users in final decision stage
Page Type 3
Use-case landing pages — "Best [Category] for [Specific Role/Industry]" — targeted ICP traffic

Every page we produce — and every existing page we identify as an opportunity — gets fully optimised for on-page SEO. This is what tells Google exactly what a page is about, who should see it, and why it deserves to rank above your competitors.

Title Tags
Written for both rankings and click-through rate — not just keyword stuffed
Meta Descriptions

04

Technical SEO

Technical SEO issues silently block rankings for months. A page can be perfectly written with perfect keyword targeting and still not rank because of a broken canonical tag or a slow LCP score. We audit your site every month — not just once — because issues appear as your site grows.

Monthly Crawl
Full site crawl every month — catches new issues before they compound
Core Web Vitals
LCP, INP, CLS — flagged, explained, and fix instructions provided
Crawlability
Blocked pages, noindex tags, robots.txt issues, sitemap accuracy
Indexation
Which pages are indexed, which aren't, and why — with exact fixes
Duplicate Content
Canonical tag issues, thin pages, near-duplicate URLs — identified and resolved
Fix Report
Every issue categorised Critical / High / Medium / Low with exact implementation instructions
Scope note: We identify all issues and provide step-by-step fix instructions. Your development team implements the fixes. For WordPress or Webflow sites, most fixes require no developer and we'll guide you through them directly.
05

Off-Page SEO — Link Building

Domain authority is built through quality backlinks from relevant websites. Without links, even perfect content struggles to rank for competitive keywords. Link building is introduced at Month 4 — once your content foundation is solid enough for links to actually move rankings. We use three legitimate methods, report on outreach activity and acquired links honestly — no guaranteed minimums, no fake numbers.

Guest Posting
Expert articles published on established SaaS and industry publications — DR 40+ sites only
Journalist Outreach
Responding to queries on Connectively and Qwoted — earned editorial mentions with links
Broken Link Building
Finding broken links on competitor resource pages and offering your content as a replacement
Link Quality Rule
DR 40+ domains relevant to your industry only. No link farms. No paid links. No PBNs.
Realistic timeline: Link building begins at Month 4, not before. The first 3 months are spent building the content foundation that makes links worthwhile. Once we start, outreach runs every month. Links typically begin arriving 4–8 weeks after outreach begins. We report on every outreach sent and every link acquired — you always see the work happening.

At the end of the 3-month minimum, we review everything together — rankings, traffic, content performance, honest assessment of what's working. You decide whether to continue on a rolling monthly basis. No pressure, no automatic renewal, no lock-in beyond the initial 3 months.
