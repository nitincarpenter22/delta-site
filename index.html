FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Talk to us →
How long before I see results? +
First ranking movements typically appear in Month 2 to 3 as Google indexes and evaluates the new pages. Meaningful organic traffic growth shows up around Month 4 to 5. Trial signups from organic start coming in reliably from Month 5 to 6 onward. SEO is not instant — any agency that promises faster results is not being honest with you.
Why a 3-month minimum contract? +
SEO is a compounding strategy — a 1-month contract would set both of us up for disappointment. Google takes 60 to 90 days to crawl, index, and begin ranking new content. Leaving before Month 3 means you exit right before the results of Month 1 work become visible. The 3-month minimum protects your investment, not ours. After Month 3, it's rolling monthly — cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
Do you work with early-stage SaaS with no existing traffic? +
Yes — in fact that's our most common engagement. Starting from zero means we build your SEO foundation correctly from Day 1 with no bad history to fix. You do need a live product with real customers and basic product-market fit. Pre-launch products are not a good fit yet.
Can you guarantee specific results? +
We guarantee our execution — never Google's algorithm. No honest agency can control that, and we won't pretend otherwise. What we do guarantee: a documented strategy built around your SaaS, full transparency on every action, and success measured in trial signups and MRR — never vanity metrics.
Do you work with SaaS companies outside India? +
Yes — Delta is remote-first and works with B2B SaaS companies globally. We communicate over email and Slack, with monthly strategy calls built to work across time zones. Our India-based pricing means you get senior-level work at a fraction of what US or UK agencies charge for the same scope at comparable Western agencies. Our pricing reflects the value delivered, not our geography.
